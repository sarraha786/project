Professional and Personal Details

Hi There!

I am majoring in Information Analysis through the School of Information and minoring in Public Policy. I am interested in studying law post grad and purusing tech law! On this page I will walk you through my professional history and some of my personal interests.

Past Work Experience

I have been involved in politics since high school. My first camapaign was Abdul El-Sayed's run for Michigan Governor in 2018. Since then, I have worked on Congresswomen Elissa Slotkin and Haley Steven's respective campaigns for congress along with Abdullah Hammoud's hisoric run for Mayor in Dearborn. I have also dabbled in political data and served on two small North Carolina campaigns as a data specialist. For the 2022 midterm election, I was an associate for a political consulting firm in Washington D.C. and got the opportunity to work on 14 different campaigns at once. Most notably, I contributed to Governor Tony Evers' big win in Wisconsin, Governor Janet Mills' win in Maine, and Congressman Andy Kim's win in New Jersey's third district.

College So Far

My interests are very diverse and I am stuyding Information Analytics because SI's approach to programming makes sense to me as someone who is interested in data as a means of civic engagement that positively affects communities. Oddly enough, my curriculum in my policy minor blends well with the more conceptual aspects of SI. It allows me to explore tech policy, data privacy, and has motivated me to pursue law so I can one day help shape tech policy that protects consumers and holds massive corporations responsible for ethical use of private information.
